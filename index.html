Attack on Titan - Notes

Origins

  • Founding Titan: First Titan, originally held by Ymir Fritz.
  • Subjects of Ymir (Eldians):
    • Descendants of Ymir Fritz.
    • Only people capable of becoming Titans.
    • The people living inside the Walls are Eldians.

Pure Titans

  • Created when an Eldian is injected with Titan spinal fluid (serum).
  • Become mindless Pure Titans and cannot revert to human.
  • Subtypes:
    • Normal Titans → attack humans instinctively.
    • Abnormal Titans → still mindless, but behave unpredictably (run fast, jump, ignore some humans, etc.).
  • Can inherit Titan powers if they eat a Titan Shifter.

Titan Shifters (The Nine Titans)

  • Descendants of the Founding Titan’s power.
  • Can shift between Titan and human form.
  • Lifespan limit: ~13 years after inheriting.
  • Power transfer method: a Pure Titan eats a Shifter → inherits that Titan power.
  • Founding Titan — Frieda Reiss → Grisha Yeager → Eren Yeager
    • (Unique: Titan control & royal restriction.)
  • Attack Titan — The Owl (Eren Kruger) → Grisha Yeager → Eren Yeager
    • Versatile, offense-oriented Titan; notable lineage of holders.
  • Female Titan — Annie Leonhart
    • Abilities: high agility, combat skill, partial hardening, a scream that can affect nearby Titans.
  • Colossal Titan — Bertholdt Hoover → Armin Arlert
    • Abilities: immense size, produces massive steam/force on transformation, causes large-scale destruction; slow but devastating.
  • Armored Titan — Reiner Braun
    • Abilities: hardened, armor-like skin; built for heavy offense/defense in close combat.
  • Beast Titan — Zeke Yeager
    • Abilities: unique, beast-like form; notable for ranged/precision attacks and a distinct fighting style.
  • Jaw Titan — Marcel Galliard → Ymir
    • Abilities: fast, agile, powerful jaws and claws for offense.
    • Marcel was chosen by Marley as the Jaw Titan for the Paradis mission.
    • Marcel was eaten by Ymir (as a Pure Titan), making her the new Jaw Titan.
    • This disrupted the Marley mission, forcing Reiner, Annie, and Bertholdt to adapt.

The Founding Titan’s Power

  • Can control other Titans and unlock unique abilities.
  • Royal Bloodline restriction: Full power can only be used by someone of royal descent (Fritz/Reiss families).

Royal Family Connections

  • Fritz Lineage (royal family outside the Walls):
    • Dina Fritz → Grisha’s first wife, royal blood. Turned into a Titan and ate Carla Yeager (Grisha’s second wife, Eren’s mother).
  • Reiss Lineage (royal family inside the Walls):
    • Rod Reiss → Head of the family.
    • Historia Reiss → Rod’s illegitimate daughter, considered of royal blood.
    • Frieda Reiss → Historia’s sister, possessed the Founding Titan before Grisha.

Ymir’s Background

  • Ymir was a normal Eldian living under Marley.
  • Eldian restorationists used her as a symbol, falsely presenting her as if she had royal blood and named here Ymir to signify her lineage with Ymir Fritz the Founding Titan.
  • She accepted the role, gaining followers and status.
  • When Marley discovered the lie:
    • She was punished, stripped of her false identity.
    • Injected with Titan serum, turning her into a Pure Titan.
    • Roamed outside the Walls for decades until she ate Marcel and inherited the Jaw Titan.

Historia’s Background

  • Historia Reiss was born as the illegitimate daughter of the Reiss family, the true royal family inside the Walls.
  • To protect her life and the royal lineage, she was hidden and raised under the alias Krista Lenz.
  • She was known for her kindness and selflessness, often putting others before herself.
  • Struggled with her identity:
    • Initially tried to conform to others’ expectations as “Krista Lenz.”
    • Later embraced her true self and her role as queen.
  • As a member of the Reiss family, she has potential connection to the Founding Titan through her bloodline.
  • Her early life was shaped by the secrecy of her birth and the sacrifices of her mother, Alma Reiss, who had a tragic and exploitative situation within the family.

Levi’s Background

  • Levi Ackerman was born in the underground city beneath the Walls.
  • His mother, Kuchel Ackerman, was a prostitute; his father is unknown.
  • Grew up in poverty, learning survival through fighting and thievery.
  • Trained by his uncle Kenny Ackerman, who taught him combat and survival skills.
  • Joined the Survey Corps, quickly becoming humanity’s strongest soldier.
  • Known for exceptional skill, speed, and strategic mind.
  • Stoic and disciplined outwardly, but deeply loyal to comrades.
  • Part of the Ackerman bloodline, granting him enhanced physical abilities and resistance to Titan powers’ influence.
